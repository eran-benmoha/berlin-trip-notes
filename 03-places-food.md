# Places to Save — Berlin

## Neighborhoods to base evenings in
- **Mitte**: central, easy logistics
- **Prenzlauer Berg**: cafés, calmer evenings
- **Kreuzberg / Neukölln**: food + bars + nightlife
- **Charlottenburg**: quieter, classic “West Berlin”
- **Your base (Wolliner Str / Bernauer Str)**: super convenient for Mauerpark + Wall memorial; great “start/end of day” area

## Near your lodging (Wolliner Str 12b)
- **Gedenkstätte Berliner Mauer (Berlin Wall Memorial)** — Bernauer Straße 119 (free)
- **Mauerpark** — Sunday flea market 10:00–18:00 (Bernauer Straße 63)
- **Transit convenience**: Tram **M10 “Wolliner Str.”** is right there (easy to Hauptbahnhof)
- **Optional nearby viewpoint/history**: **Flak Tower Humboldthain** (guided tours by Berliner Unterwelten)
